Image Compression

Interactive walkthroughs of how real image formats shrink photos — what they keep, what they throw away, and why. Best read in order: PNG extends the lossless coding ideas to images, JPEG introduces lossy compression and the DCT, and WebP refines JPEG. Each page runs the actual compression in your browser.

1 · Lossless

PNG

Filtering/prediction + DEFLATE (LZ77 + Huffman). See residuals collapse the entropy — a direct sequel to the lossless coding demos.

● Live
2 · Lossy

JPEG

Color, the DCT (a cousin of the Fourier transform), and quantization — plus a live quality lab and an 8×8 block explorer.

● Live
3 · Modern

WebP

Builds on JPEG with intra-prediction and a better entropy coder — encode WebP vs JPEG vs PNG live and compare bytes.

● Live
4 · Beyond

Modern & Neural

AVIF, JPEG XL, and learned codecs — a live JPEG-vs-WebP rate–distortion curve and how neural compression works.
